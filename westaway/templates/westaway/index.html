This app is used as a log of all the times I've been to see West Didsbury & Chorlton AFC play. The intention was mostly as a reminder for myself and an opportunity to learn how to use Django but if anyone else stumbles upon this then I hope you enjoy it.

If I remember rightly, the first season I saw a West game was 2017/18 but it could have been 2018/19. I was definitely watching by the time City of Liverpool won the league and West were relegated. Many home games from that time though to 2023/24 are missing.

I'm casually making some additional changes to this site to make it a little easier to use (mostly for myself) but please don't judge me on my graphic design.
